Pro Evolution Soccer 2017

PES 2017 uses Konamis Fox Engine once again and this time we are expecting it to turn up in full on PC. Therefore PES 2017 will require some more powerful hardware this year, to get it to run smoothly at high graphics settings on a 1080p screen resolution. PES 2017 will still not need a modern computer hardware beast, just be prepared for a jump up in requirements compared to last year. Check back for more PES2017 PC info as it comes out.
Despite all of Konamis troubles, the Japanese publisher still has one ace up its sleeve with the beloved Pro Evolution Soccer franchise. PES 2017 brings the 2016-17 football season to soccer fans around the globe, no doubt with a suite of new features, modes, and the greatest footballers in the world.
