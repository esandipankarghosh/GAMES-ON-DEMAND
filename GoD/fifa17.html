FIFA 17

Fifa 17 will require a similarly powered computer to its predecessor last year. We expect it will run with good frame rates at high graphics settings on 1080p with a Intel core i5 2550K or an AMD FX-8120 and around a Nvidia GTX 570 or a HD 6970 and 8 GB system memory. We will update FIFA 17 system requirement data as more information is released.
EA Sports long-running soccer series is set to continue its dominance this year with the launch of FIFA 17. Packing in all the latest roster updates from the 2016-17 season, FIFA 17 is sure to be one of the most authentic football games out there and lets see how Fifa 2017 vs PES 2017 stacks up this year.
