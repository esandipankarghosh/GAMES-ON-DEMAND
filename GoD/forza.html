Forza Horizon 3

Should Forza Horizon 3 come to PC expect it to have very high system requirements. Being a first-party Microsoft title means it will be available in Windows 10 only and will require DirectX 12. Expect to need a modern, mid-high end card such as GTX 960 or R9 380 to get Forza Horizon 3 running at a decent frame rate.
Forza Horizon 3 was announced at E3 2016 as a cross-platform and cross-play game for both Xbox One and PC. It is an arcade racer which encourages off-road racing.
